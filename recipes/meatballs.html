Meatballs

Description:

Ingredients

  • 1000g ground pork
  • 500g pasta (Spaghetti)
  • 800g tomato sauce
  • 3 medium onions
  • 3 carrots
  • salt, pepper, paprika, oregano, basil, orange peel, honey
  • 3-4 cloves of garlic

  1. mix the meat with pepper, salt and paprika and form it into ~3 cm balls
  2. fry the meatballs in a pan until the have a nice brovn crust
  3. when done, put them on a plate
  4. put 5l of salted water on the stove
  5. dice the onions, grate the carrots and put them in the pan with the fat from the meatballs
  6. add a bit (around 2dl) of water to the pan and put the lid on it
  7. when the onion is soft and the carrot is sweet out the garlic in the pan(minced or diced)
  8. when the pasta water is boiling put in the pasta and boil until al dante
  9. pour in the tomato sauce the orange peels and the spices and let it simmer for a few minutes
  10. put the meatballs into the pan
  11. cut the heat and take out the orange peels
  12. put on plate, grate cheese on top and serve

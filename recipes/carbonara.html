Carbonara

Description:

Carbonara is a dish made with eggs, cheese, pasta, bacon and pepper.

In the original recipe ther are no other ingredients but i like to add some mushroom and garlic

It's a simple dish, but quite sifficult to master.

Ingredients

  • 200g Bacon
  • 300g pasta (Spaghetti or tagliatelle)
  • 4 eggs
  • 100g pecorino romano cheese
  • salt, pepper

    • optional:

  • 3-4 cloves of garlic
  • 4-5 heads of mushroom

  1. put 3l of salted water on the stove
  2. dice the bacon, slice the mushrooms and put them in a pan
  3. turn the heat under the pan to medium
  4. separate 2 egg yolks and mix them with the other 2 whole eggs
  5. grate the cheese and add it to the eggs with some ground pepper and mix well
  6. when the pasta water is boiling put in the pasta and boil until al dante
  7. when the bacon and the mushroom is geting brown add in the garlic(minced or cut in small pieces)
  8. when the garlic is geting a golden color cut the heat, add the pasta with a few tabelspoons of pasta water and mix well
  9. after the mix cooled a bit pour in the egg mixture and quickly mix it together
  10. put on plate, grate cheese on top and serve

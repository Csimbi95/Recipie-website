Bolognese

Description:

Ingredients

  • 1000g minced pork and/or beef
  • 500g pasta (Spaghetti)
  • 3-4 medium onions
  • 3-4 carrots
  • 3-4 cloves of garlic
  • 800g tomato sauce
  • salt, pepper, oregano, basil

    • optional:

  • a little bit of nutmeg
  • 3-4 heads of mushroom
  • 1-2 tablespoon of honey

  1. put 5l of salted water on the stove
  2. dice the onions, grate the carrots and put them in a pan
  3. turn the heat under the pan to medium
  4. add a bit (around 2dl) of water to the pan and put the lid on it
  5. when the onion is soft and the carrot is sweet out the garlic in the pan(minced or diced)
  6. when the pasta water is boiling put in the pasta and boil until al dante
  7. put the meat and the shrooms into the pan
  8. let it boil most of the water off from the meat
  9. pour in the tomato sauce and the spices
  10. mix well and let it heat up to a boil
  11. cut the heat
  12. put on plate, grate cheese on top and serve
